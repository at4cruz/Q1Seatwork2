Characters in Noli Me Tangere

Maria Clara She is the fiancee of the main protangonist, Juan Crisostomo Ibarra. She is the daughter of Capitan Tiago and is a Spanish mestiza.
Padre Damaso He is a friar and the godfather of Maria Clara. He seems to be a dramatic person.
Crisostomo Ibarra He is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra and a Spanish mestizo. He is also the fiancee of Maria Clara.
Capitan Tiago He is the father of Maria Clara. He is also a business man.
Don Rafael Ibarra He is the father of Crisostomo Ibarra. He died early on in the story.
